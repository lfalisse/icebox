Monarch
Composed of complicated, intricate, yet organic backgrounds and textured wing-like subjects, 'Monarch' is an abstract collection of images slightly resembling butterflies in nature. The subject is often very hard to distinguish from its background due in part to the clashing colors. The background closely resembles a field or bed of flowers and wildlife.
